HOW IT WORKS

SIGNALSCAN has two modes. REVERSAL detects when a trend is about to flip. CONTINUATION detects when a healthy trend is likely to keep going.

Price Action Structure25% — BOTH MODES

Raw price behavior — higher highs/lows, breakouts on volume, 52-week proximity, recent momentum.

MACD Momentum Shift30% of indicators

Scores only when histogram is actively changing direction.

Stochastic Cross25% of indicators

%K crossing above %D from below 25 = full bull. Above 75 = full bear.

RSI Exhaustion20% of indicators

Only scores at true extremes. RSI 35–65 scores exactly zero.

Bollinger Band Bounce15% of indicators

Price at lower band = full bull. Upper band = full bear. Mid-band = zero.

EMA Reclaim / Reject10% of indicators

Only scores when price is actively crossing an EMA (within 2%).

Price Action Structure25% — BOTH MODES

Same as Reversal — raw price structure always votes first.

EMA Stack + Slope30% of indicators

EMA20 > EMA50 = healthy bull. Pullback to EMA20 = ideal buy-the-dip zone.

MACD Expansion25% of indicators

Histogram positive AND expanding = full bull. Shrinking = trend losing steam.

RSI Momentum Zone20% of indicators

RSI 50–65 = bull momentum zone. RSI 35–50 in bear structure = downtrend intact.

OBV Trend15% of indicators

OBV rising + volume above average = institutions participating.

Bollinger Bandwidth10% of indicators

Bandwidth >8% = trend strengthening. <4% = consolidating.
