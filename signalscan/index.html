🐂 WHAT IS A GOLDEN BULL?

A Golden Bull signal fires only when both the Reversal engine and the Continuation engine independently return a BULLISH verdict on the same stock. Two completely separate algorithms must agree — making it the highest-conviction signal in the system.

Reversal Engine ✓Must be BULLISH

Detects momentum shifts at extremes — oversold RSI, MACD histogram reversing, Bollinger Band bounces, Stochastic crosses from below 25.

Continuation Engine ✓Must be BULLISH

Confirms the trend is structurally sound — EMA20 above EMA50, rising OBV, MACD histogram expanding in positive territory, RSI in the 50–65 momentum zone.

Conviction ScoreCOMBINED

The displayed conviction % is the average of both engines' raw scores. Higher = stronger agreement between the two systems. 70%+ is considered high conviction.

Why It's RareSIGNAL QUALITY

Most stocks pass one engine or neither. A genuine Golden Bull — where the stock is simultaneously showing reversal momentum AND healthy trend structure — typically appears in fewer than 5–10% of scanned tickers.
